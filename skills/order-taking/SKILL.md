# Order Taking Skill

## Triggers

- "I want to order"
- "Can I get"
- "I'd like to buy"
- "Order for"
- "Order some"

## Flow

1. Customer mentions wanting to order
2. Ask what they'd like OR confirm items if listed
3. Ask about quantities
4. Check if delivery or pickup
5. For delivery: get address
6. Calculate total price
7. Confirm entire order
8. Thank them

## Examples

Customer: "I want to order"
Agent: "Great! What would you like to order today?"

Customer: "2 butter chicken and 1 garlic naan"
Agent: "Perfect! 2x Butter Chicken and 1x Garlic Naan. For here or to go?"

Customer: "For delivery"
Agent: "Sure! What's your delivery address?"

## Important

- Always confirm items before finalizing
- Repeat the full order back to customer
- Ask if they need anything else
