# Complaint Handling Skill

## Triggers

- "I'm unhappy"
- "disappointed"
- "terrible"
- "not happy"
- "complaint"
- "worst"
- "angry"

## Flow

1. Listen and empathize
2. Don't get defensive
3. Ask for details (what happened, when)
4. Apologize sincerely
5. Offer solution
6. If serious, escalate to owner
7. Follow up

## Responses

- "I'm truly sorry to hear that..."
- "I understand your frustration..."
- "Thank you for bringing this to our attention..."

## Important

- Never argue with customers
- Always apologize first
- Use handle_complaint tool
- Escalate if: food safety, violence, medical emergency, threats
- High urgency = auto-escalate to owner
